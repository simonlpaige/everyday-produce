About the stand

Konrad has been doing this a long time.

He started selling produce in Waldo because he liked it. Fruit and vegetables, in season, priced fair. It is a smaller operation than you might expect - more personal. He shows up most days and knows what came in that week.

The stand has been in the neighborhood since 1985. The idea has not changed: sell good produce to the people who live here. No app. No loyalty points. Just a tent and whatever is ready right now.
